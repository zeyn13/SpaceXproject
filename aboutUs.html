The Space Exploration Technologies Corporation (SpaceX) is an American spacecraft manufacturer, launcher, and satellite communications company headquartered in Hawthorne, California.

The company was founded in 2002 by Elon Musk with the goal of reducing space transportation costs and to colonize Mars.


What makes us different?

SpaceX is the first private company to develop a liquid-propellant rocket that has reached orbit.
